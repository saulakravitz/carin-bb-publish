CodeSystem: C4BB Adjudication

Summary

Defining URL:http://hl7.org/fhir/us/carin-bb/CodeSystem/C4BBAdjudication
Version:0.1.4
Name:C4BBAdjudication ("C4BB Adjudication")
Status:Active as of 2020-09-22T23:20:47+00:00
Definition:

Describes the various amount fields used when payers receive and adjudicate a claim. It complements the values defined in http://terminology.hl7.org/CodeSystem/adjudication.
