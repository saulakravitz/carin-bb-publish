Resource Profile: C4BBCoverage

Defining URL:http://hl7.org/fhir/us/carin-bb/StructureDefinition/C4BB-Coverage
Version:0.1.4
Name:C4BBCoverage
Title:C4BB Coverage
Status:Active as of 2020-10-07T13:09:14+00:00
Definition:

Data that reflect a payer's coverage that was effective as of the date of service or the date of admission of the claim will be added to the Coverage Profile overview.

Publisher:HL7 Financial Management Working Group
Source Resource:XML / JSON / Turtle

The official URL for this profile is: 

http://hl7.org/fhir/us/carin-bb/StructureDefinition/C4BB-Coverage
