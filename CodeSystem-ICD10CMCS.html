International Classification of Diseases, Tenth Revision, Clinical Modification)

Publisher:HL7 Financial Management Working Group

Copyright:

The ICD-10-CM code set is maintained by the National Center for Health Statistics (NCHS) of the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) for use in the United States. It is based on ICD-10, which was developed by the World Health Organization (WHO) and is used internationally as a medical classification. The code set is owned by the NCHS-CDC and is available for use.
